Chad Campbell Surges Into Tie at Masters
AUGUSTA, Ga.   -- Chad Campbell, who opened with a 71, surged into a tie with Vijay Singh on day two of the Masters on Friday with three straight birdies beginning at No. 12, pushing the Texan's score to 6 under. But Singh, who won the 2000 Masters and was among the last golfers to tee off, kept a share of the lead with a birdie on his first hole....
