Mickelson, O'Hern Go 3-Under at Masters
AUGUSTA, Ga.   -- Day two of the Masters: Phil Mickelson had a morning tee time for the second round and quickly got to 3 under with a birdie on the second hole. Nick O'Hern also was at 3 under, while Sergia Garcia, perhaps the best player never to win a major, reached 2 under with birdies on two of his first three holes. Vijay Singh and Tiger Woods are set to tee off in the afternoon....
