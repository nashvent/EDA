Streaking Canadiens Push Toward Playoffs
Chris Higgins and the Montreal Canadiens are surging toward the playoffs - and up the Eastern Conference standings - with the franchise's longest winning streak in more than 13 years. Higgins scored the go-ahead goal and David Aebischer made 44 saves to help Montreal win its eighth straight with a 5-3 victory over the short-handed and road-weary Ottawa Senators on Thursday night....
