Tiger Is Favorite As Masters Starts Today
AUGUSTA, Ga.   -- The Masters golf tournament, plagued by rain in recent years, began Thursday morning under blue skies and a brilliant sun. Rod Pampling, Lucas Glover, Ben Crane and Miguel Angel Jimenez had early birdies, as did a couple of aging former champions - Ben Crenshaw and 70-year-old Gary Player. Tiger Woods was scheduled to tee off at 10:23 a.m. EDT....
