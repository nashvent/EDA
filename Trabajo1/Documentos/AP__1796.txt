Duke Lacrosse Team Known for Its Swagger
DURHAM, N.C.   -- At Duke University, they like to say there's only one real fraternity on campus: LAX, shorthand for lacrosse. Long before the university was rocked by allegations that lacrosse players raped a stripper during an off-campus party March 14, Duke's highly ranked team had a reputation for swagger and a powerful sense of entitlement. Now administrators are starting to wonder whether they put up with it for too long....
