Bomb kills 10 at Iraq holy site
A car bomb detonated near a shrine in Najaf, Iraq, today, killing 10 people and wounding 39, police said. Also, an Iraqi considered the prime suspect in the 2005 kidnapping of an Italian journalist has been captured, multinational military forces said in a statement. Muhammed Hila Hammad Ubaydi has strong ties to terror leader Abu Musab Al-Zarqawi, considered the head of al Qaeda in Iraq.
