Tornadoes pound Nashville area
A line of severe thunderstorms and multiple tornadoes swept parts of four states on Friday, with reports of damage north and west of Nashville, Tennessee, authorities said. Dickson County Mayor Linda Frasier said: "We have homes damaged. We do have some injuries already being reported." She added: "We are working feverishly to get through to the people to be able to assess the damage and get to anybody who will be in need of shelter or whatever else their needs may be."
