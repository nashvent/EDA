Anthony Gives Nuggets OT Win Over Lakers
DENVER   -- Kobe Bryant can have the scoring title. Carmelo Anthony is cementing himself as the NBA's best in the clutch. Anthony's jumper with 3.8 seconds left in overtime gave the Denver Nuggets a 110-108 win over the Los Angeles Lakers on Thursday night. It was the eighth time this season he's made a big shot in the waning seconds....
