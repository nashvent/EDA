Flyers Top Sabres, Clinch Playoff Spot
BUFFALO, N.Y.   -- The Philadelphia Flyers gained more than just a playoff berth against the Buffalo Sabres. After the Flyers clinched a postseason spot Friday night with a 4-2 victory over the Sabres, Philadelphia coach Ken Hitchcock and his players agreed that a swagger - especially against potential playoff opponent Buffalo - is also starting to emerge....
