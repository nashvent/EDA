"Meltdown" rivals facing a mammoth task at box office
LOS ANGELES (Hollywood Reporter) - Although four new wide releases will try to climb the box-office heights this weekend, they are facing a veritable Mount Everest in 20th Century Fox's "Ice Age: The Meltdown," which established itself last weekend as the first bona fide blockbuster of the year when it opened to $68 million.
