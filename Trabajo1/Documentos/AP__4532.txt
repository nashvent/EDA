Nowitzki Lifts Mavs Over Spurs in Showdown
SAN ANTONIO   -- Dirk Nowitzki's big second half moved the Dallas Mavericks closer to first place in the Western Conference. Nowitzki scored 19 of his 30 points in the second half to lead the Mavericks over the Spurs 92-86 in a showdown of the top two teams in the Western Conference on Friday night....
